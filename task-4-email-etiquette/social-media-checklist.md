5 DO's and DON'Ts of social media as a college student:

DO's:-

 1. Think before you post — Ask yourself: Would I be comfortable if a future employer, professor, or family member saw this? Once something is online, it can persist   forever through screenshots and archives, even after deletion.

 2. Use privacy settings wisely and well.

 3. Build a positive personal brand by follow accounts that resonate with your interests and goals, and engage in healthy discussions.

 4. Google yourself periodically — Search your own name to check what's publicly visible.

 5. Take regular breaks from social media.

DON'Ts:-

 1. Don't post your location or personal details.
 
 2. Don't post in anger or impulse.
 
 3. Don't engage in cyberbullying or hate speech.
 
 4. Don't trust strangers online blindly.
 
 5. Don't overshare content that questions your character.
